Help

  • New users can sign up to pay CC charges via Credit/Debit cards or Net Banking.
  • After registration, an email is sent for validation.
  • Users must log in with their password for making payments.
  • Consumer ID/User ID cannot be modified after registration.
  • Users can add up to 24 Consumer Numbers for payments.
  • Downloadable e-receipts are available.
  • Users can update their email ID and personal details.
  • Regularly change passwords for security.
  • A "Forgot Password" link helps retrieve lost passwords.
